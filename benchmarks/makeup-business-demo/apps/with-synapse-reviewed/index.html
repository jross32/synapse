About Us

Glow Studio was founded by Sofia Reyes, a licensed esthetician and makeup artist with over a decade of experience in bridal, editorial, and everyday beauty. We believe every client deserves to feel radiant and confident, no matter the occasion — whether you're saying "I do" or simply treating yourself. Our studio is a calm, welcoming space in the heart of the city where artistry and genuine self-care come beautifully together.
